Resources I've used:

My personal thoughts

Learning html so far has been a fairly easy process. While I have learned some parts of other programming languages, this I believe this would still be an easy process for someone who's brand new. I have enjoyed learning how everything works together and creates the general structure of a web page. It's interesting to see something you've looked at for years be so simple the whole time

What I have learned

I have learned copious amounts of entities, tags, attributes, ect. From forming a basic semantic layout of a webpage to adding special characters, all of it has been productive knowledge in my process. While I know there is still a lot to learn, I know this is still a big step in the right direction.
